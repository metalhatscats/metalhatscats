# MetalHatsCats

Workflow systems, internal tools, AI-ready datasets, and practical products for complex work.

MetalHatsCats builds structured systems that have to survive production reality: operational workflows, internal tooling, machine-readable datasets, and content systems designed for search, retrieval, and reuse.

## What we build

- Workflow systems for real operational teams
- Internal tools and review surfaces
- Open structured datasets and knowledge assets
- AI-ready publishing and retrieval layers
- Practical automation for complex delivery environments

## Selected repositories

- [metalhatscats-datasets](https://github.com/metalhatscats/metalhatscats-datasets) - open structured datasets for citation, search visibility, and AI retrieval
- [bonihua-datasets](https://github.com/metalhatscats/bonihua-datasets) - Russian-language open datasets for Chinese language learning
- [brainlighter.app](https://github.com/metalhatscats/brainlighter.app) - habit tracker and daily routine planner
- [metkagram](https://github.com/metalhatscats/metkagram) - annotated flashcards and learning documents
- [CBT-cards](https://github.com/metalhatscats/CBT-cards) - CBT cards and related learning assets
- [verbsinuse.app](https://github.com/metalhatscats/verbsinuse.app) - language-learning product focused on verb usage

## Current focus

- Enterprise workflow tooling
- Agentic systems and practical automation
- Dataset-first publishing
- Search-ready and AI-ready product surfaces
